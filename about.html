About Me

Hi, I’m Zoe – an urban geographer, spatial analyst, and explorer of the intersections between cities, care, and creativity.

🌍 What I Do

I work across GIS, urban planning, and environmental resilience. I love turning data into stories and using maps to amplify marginalized voices.

📚 What Drives Me

I care about aging, accessibility, displacement, and disaster justice. I want to build cities where dignity, memory, and mobility coexist.

🐾 Who Inspires Me

My cat – patient, soft, curious. She reminds me that slowing down is a form of resistance, and that everything begins with attention.
